Gameplay


This is a two player game where each player will have to correctly guess a Jumbled Word.



Step 1 :- Click on the Jumbled Words tab
Step 2 :- Enter names of both the players and click on 'Start Game'
Step 3 :- Choose the scenario you wish to play i.e. Formal, Informal, Public Speaking, Any
Step 4 :- On choosing the scenario the game shall start for player 1.
Step 5 :- A randomised word shall be displayed on the screen.
Step 6 :- Player 1 will have to guess the correct spelling of the word.
Step 7 :- If the guess is right, 1 point will be awarded. No point shall be there for a wrong answer.
Step 8 :- The incorrectly guessed word shall not pass to player 2.
Step 9 :- A new randomised word shall be displayed on the screen.
Step 10 :- Player 2 will have to guess the correct spelling of the word.
Step 11 :- If the guess is right, 1 point will be awarded. No point shall be there for a wrong answer.
Step 12 :- The incorrectly guessed word shall not pass to player 1.
Step 13 :- In the same sequence, all rounds shall be played
Step 14 :- The player first scoring 3 points shall be declared as the winner.
Step 15 :- To start a new game click on Jumbled Words
